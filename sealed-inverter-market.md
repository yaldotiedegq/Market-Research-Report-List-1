<p><p>The sealed inverter market comprises two primary types: fully sealed and semi-sealed inverters. Fully sealed inverters are hermetically enclosed, offering maximum protection against dust, moisture, and environmental contaminants, making them ideal for harsh conditions. In contrast, semi-sealed inverters have some level of exposure to the environment while still providing moderate protection. They balance cost and durability, making them suitable for less extreme settings. Both types cater to diverse applications in renewable energy, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
