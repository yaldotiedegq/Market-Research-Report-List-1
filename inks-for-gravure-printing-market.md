<p><p>The inks for gravure printing market is categorized into three main types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly, offering low volatility and quick drying times. Solvent-based inks provide vibrant colors and enhanced adhesion, ideal for various substrates but pose environmental challenges. UV-cured inks utilize ultraviolet light for rapid curing, resulting in durability and a high-gloss finish, making them suitable for specialized applications. Each type caters to diverse printing needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
