<p><p>Microcatheters are small, flexible tubes used to deliver therapeutic or diagnostic agents directly to specific areas within the body, particularly in the vascular system. They facilitate minimally invasive procedures, allowing for targeted treatment while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. These devices are increasingly utilized in various medical applications such as interventional radiology, cardiology, and neurology, driving demand in the healthcare sector.</p><p>The Microcatheters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period.
