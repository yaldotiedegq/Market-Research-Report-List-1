<p><p>The miniature absolute encoders market comprises two main types: single-turn and multi-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders measure position within a single rotation, offering high accuracy in compact applications. They are ideal for systems requiring precise control over limited rotation. Multi-turn encoders, on the other hand, track multiple rotations, enabling the identification of absolute position over greater ranges. These are essential for applications where complete rotational data and high precision are crucial, such as robotics and automation systems.</p></p>
