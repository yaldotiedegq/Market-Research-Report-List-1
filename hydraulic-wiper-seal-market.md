<p><strong>The Hydraulic Wiper Seal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic wiper seal market is divided into single-acting and double-acting types. Single-acting wiper seals are designed to protect hydraulic cylinders by preventing contamination from entering during the extension stroke, ideal for applications with only one direction of motion. On the other hand, double-acting wiper seals provide protection during both extension and retraction, making them suitable for dynamic applications requiring bidirectional movement. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements of the hydraulic system's operational dynamics.</p></p>
