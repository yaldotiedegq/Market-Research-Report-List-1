<p><p>The automotive-grade comparator IC market includes three main types: quad voltage comparators, dual voltage comparators, and simple voltage comparators. Quad voltage comparators integrate four comparators in a single package, ideal for complex automotive applications requiring multiple voltage comparisons. Dual voltage comparators offer two comparators, suitable for standard automotive circuits with fewer requirements. Simple voltage comparators provide a basic functionality for straightforward voltage detection tasks. Each type caters to different levels of complexity and design requirements in automotive electronics.</p></p>
