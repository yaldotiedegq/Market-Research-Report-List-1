<p><strong>The Drinking Fountains (Water Dispensers) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Dispenser or Bottled Water Cooler(BWC)</li><li>Point-of-Use(POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drinking fountains and water dispensers market can be categorized into two main types: Bottled Water Dispenser (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) systems. BWC units utilize large bottled water supplies, typically requiring periodic replacement of the bottles, making them suitable for locations without plumbing. In contrast, POU systems connect directly to a water source, providing a convenient and continuous supply of filtered water, often installed in office or residential settings. Both types serve to meet hydration needs in various environments.</p></p>
