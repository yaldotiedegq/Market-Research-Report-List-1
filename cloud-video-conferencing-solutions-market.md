<p><ul><li>Scalable Video Coding (SVC) Type</li><li>Advanced Video Coding (AVC) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud video conferencing solutions are categorized into two main types: Scalable Video Coding (SVC) and Advanced Video Coding (AVC). SVC enables dynamic adjustment of video quality based on network capacity and device capabilities, enhancing user experience without interruption. In contrast, AVC provides high-quality video streams but lacks the same flexibility in adapting to varying conditions. Both types cater to different user needs, with SVC favored for larger, diverse audiences, while AVC is often used for high-fidelity applications in stable environments.</p></p>
