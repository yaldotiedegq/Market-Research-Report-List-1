<p><ul><li>Autoclave Process</li><li>Tubular Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MDPE (Medium-Density Polyethylene) material market comprises two primary processes: the autoclave process and the tubular process. The autoclave process involves using high-pressure vessels to polymerize ethylene, resulting in a more uniform molecular weight and properties suitable for various applications. In contrast, the tubular process utilizes a continuous flow system, providing high production efficiency and lower energy costs. Both processes cater to different needs in sectors like packaging, construction, and piping, each offering distinct advantages in quality and production scalability.</p></p>
