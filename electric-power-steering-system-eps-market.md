<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering System (EPS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li><li>DP-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Steering (EPS) systems enhance vehicle maneuverability through electronically controlled steering assistance. The market includes several types: </p><p>1. C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering) integrates motor within the steering column, offering compact design.</p><p>2. P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering) positions the motor at the pinion gear, providing a direct steering response.</p><p>3. R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering) features the motor on the steering rack, improving efficiency.</p><p>4. DP-EPS (Dual Pinion Electric Power Steering) utilizes two motors for enhanced performance and precision, catering to diverse vehicle dynamics.</p></p>
