<p><p>The superfinishing stones market is categorized into four main types based on the abrasive material used. Aluminum oxide stones are known for their durability and versatility, suitable for a variety of materials. Silicon carbide stones are more aggressive, ideal for hard materials and providing a finer finish. Cubic boron nitride stones offer exceptional hardness and stability, primarily used for finishing ferrous metals. Diamond stones, the hardest option, deliver superior performance for ultra-precise applications, especially on tough materials.</p></p>
