Open gantry robots feature a structure without walls or barriers, allowing for greater flexibility and accessibility in the workspace, making them suitable for tasks requiring extensive maneuverability. In contrast, closed gantry robots are designed with enclosed structures, providing enhanced protection for components and stability in operation. This design is advantageous in environments needing precision and safety, such as manufacturing or assembly lines.</p></p>
