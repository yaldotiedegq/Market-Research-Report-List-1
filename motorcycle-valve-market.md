<p><p>The motorcycle valve market encompasses two primary types: engine valves and wheel valves. Engine valves control the airflow in and out of the combustion chamber, playing a critical role in engine performance and efficiency. Wheel valves, on the other hand, manage air pressure within tires, ensuring proper inflation and safety. Both types are essential for optimal motorcycle function, with engine valves influencing power output and fuel consumption, while wheel valves are vital for handling, stability, and overall riding safety.</p></p>
