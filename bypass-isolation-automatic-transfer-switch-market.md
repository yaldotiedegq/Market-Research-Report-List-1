<p><p>The Bypass Isolation Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) market comprises two key types: Open Transition and Closed Transition. Open Transition switches transfer power between sources with a momentary disconnect, ensuring a simple and cost-effective solution. In contrast, Closed Transition switches allow for a seamless transfer between sources, maintaining power continuity without interruption. Both types are crucial for critical applications, providing reliable power management while enhancing system resilience and minimizing downtime during maintenance or power outages.</p></p>
