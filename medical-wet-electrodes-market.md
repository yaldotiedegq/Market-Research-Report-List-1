<p><p>The medical wet electrodes market is segmented into disposable and reusable types. Disposable electrodes are designed for single-use, providing convenience, reducing the risk of cross-contamination, and ensuring optimal hygiene during medical procedures. In contrast, reusable electrodes are designed for multiple uses, making them cost-effective and environmentally friendly over time, though they require proper cleaning and maintenance between uses. Both types play crucial roles in various medical applications, including electrocardiograms (ECGs) and electromyography (EMG), catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
