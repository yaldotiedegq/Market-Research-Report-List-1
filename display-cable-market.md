<p><strong>The Display Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The display cable market can be categorized into passive and active types. Passive display cables transmit signals without additional processing, relying on the source device's power and capabilities, making them simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, active display cables contain built-in electronics that amplify and convert signals, allowing them to support longer distances and higher resolutions. This results in enhanced performance for demanding applications, such as high-definition video and gaming, but at a higher cost and complexity compared to passive options.</p></p>
