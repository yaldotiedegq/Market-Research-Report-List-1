<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump Heart Lung Machine</li><li>Double Roller Pump Heart Lung Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart-lung machine market is segmented into two main types: single roller pump and double roller pump systems. Single roller pump heart-lung machines utilize a single roller mechanism to circulate blood, providing a simpler design and potentially lower costs. In contrast, double roller pump machines employ two rollers, offering enhanced flow stability and reduced shear stress on blood, making them suitable for complex surgical procedures. Both types are crucial for cardiac surgeries, ensuring adequate blood circulation and oxygenation during operations.</p></p>
