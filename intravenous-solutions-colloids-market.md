<p><p>Intravenous solutions in the colloids market are primarily classified into two types: Peripheral Parenteral Nutrition (PPN) and Total Parenteral Nutrition (TPN). PPN is administered through peripheral veins for patients who can tolerate some oral intake but require additional nutrients. TPN delivers comprehensive nutrition through central veins for patients with severe digestive disorders or those unable to consume food. Both solutions provide essential macronutrients and micronutrients, ensuring proper nutrition for patients unable to achieve this through traditional means.</p></p>
