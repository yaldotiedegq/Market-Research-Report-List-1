<p><p>In-line magnetic flowmeters are essential for measuring the flow of conductive liquids in various industries. The DC magnetic flowmeter operates using a direct current to create a magnetic field, which induces a voltage proportional to the flow rate. In contrast, induction magnetic flowmeters utilize alternating currents to generate a magnetic field, providing precise flow measurements. Both types are highly accurate and reliable, making them ideal for applications in water treatment, chemical processing, and food and beverage industries.</p></p>
