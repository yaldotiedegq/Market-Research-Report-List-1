<p><strong>The Construction Glass Curtain Wall Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unitised Type</li><li>Stick Built Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction glass curtain wall market consists of two primary types: unitised and stick built. The unitised type features prefabricated panels assembled off-site, allowing for faster installation and improved quality control. Each panel contains multiple glass sections, enhancing efficiency. In contrast, the stick built type is constructed on-site, where individual components are assembled, requiring skilled labor and more time. This method offers flexibility in design but can lead to increased labor costs and longer project timelines. Both types cater to varying architectural and budgetary needs.</p></p>
