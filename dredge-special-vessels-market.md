<p><strong>The Dredge Special Vessels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trailing Suction Hopper Dredger</li><li>Cutter-suction Dredge</li><li>Trailing Suction Dredge</li><li>Bucket Dredger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dredge Special Vessels market includes various types of dredgers utilized for excavation and material relocation in marine environments. Trailing Suction Hopper Dredgers (TSHD) feature a suction pipe to collect sediment, storing it in onboard hoppers for disposal. Cutter-suction dredges use rotating blades to cut through material, pumping it through pipelines. Trailing Suction Dredges combine features of TSHD and traditional dredging techniques. Bucket dredgers employ mechanical buckets to scoop up sediments, suitable for shallow or compacted materials. Each type serves specific dredging needs and environments.</p></p>
