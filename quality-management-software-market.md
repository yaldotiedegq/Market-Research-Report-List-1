<p><p>Quality Management Software (QMS) is categorized into three main market types: Cloud-Based, Web-Based, and On-Premise. **Cloud-Based QMS** offers accessibility and scalability through online hosting, allowing users to manage quality processes from anywhere. **Web-Based QMS** operates similarly but focuses on browser access without requiring installation, enabling real-time collaboration. **On-Premise QMS** involves deploying software within an organization’s infrastructure, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Each type caters to different business needs and technological environments.</p></p>
