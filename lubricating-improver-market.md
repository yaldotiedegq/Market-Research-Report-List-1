<p><strong>The Lubricating Improver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acidic Lubricating Improver</li><li>Non-Acidic Lubricating Improver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lubricating improver market is categorized into two main types: acidic and non-acidic lubricating improvers. Acidic lubricating improvers are characterized by their ability to enhance the performance of lubricants by providing better anti-wear properties and oxidation stability, often utilized in high-performance applications. Non-acidic lubricating improvers, on the other hand, focus on reducing friction and enhancing fluidity without introducing acidity, making them suitable for a broader range of applications, particularly in consumer automotive lubricants. Both types play crucial roles in improving lubrication efficiency and equipment longevity.</p></p>
