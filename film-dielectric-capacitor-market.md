<p><p>The film dielectric capacitor market is primarily categorized into two types: AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are designed to handle alternating current applications, exhibiting high insulation resistance and stability under varying voltage conditions. They are commonly used in power electronics and audio applications. In contrast, DC film capacitors are optimized for direct current applications, offering high capacitance values and low losses. They play a crucial role in energy storage and filtering applications in various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
