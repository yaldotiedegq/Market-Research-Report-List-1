<p><p>The gonorrhea testing kits market includes several types of diagnostic methods. Nucleic Acid Amplification (NAA) tests detect the genetic material of the bacteria with high sensitivity. Gram Stain involves staining and microscopic examination of samples to identify the bacteria. Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA) detects antibodies related to gonorrhea. Gonorrhea Culture allows for the growth of the bacteria from specimens to confirm infection. Rapid Tests provide quick results, typically using immunological methods for faster diagnosis and treatment initiation.</p></p>
