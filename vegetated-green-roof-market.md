<p><ul><li>Extensive (Shallow Depth) Modules</li><li>Semi-Intensive (Intermediate Depth) Modules</li><li>Intensive (Maximum Depth) Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vegetated green roof market consists of three types based on soil depth and maintenance requirements. Extensive modules feature shallow soil layers, supporting drought-resistant plants, and require minimal maintenance, making them cost-effective. Semi-intensive modules offer intermediate soil depth, allowing a wider variety of plants and moderate maintenance needs. Intensive modules, with maximum soil depth, support diverse landscapes, including larger plants and recreational use, but demand higher maintenance and structural support. Each type serves different environmental and aesthetic purposes.</p></p>
