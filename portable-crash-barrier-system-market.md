<p><p>The Portable Crash Barrier System market is segmented into three types: rigid, semi-rigid, and flexible barriers. Rigid barriers are designed to withstand high impact forces, providing maximum protection but with limited deflection. Semi-rigid barriers offer a balance by absorbing some impact energy while allowing slight deformation, enhancing safety without sacrificing structural integrity. Flexible barriers are designed to absorb energy through deformation, minimizing damage and injuries, making them suitable for varying road conditions. Each type serves specific safety and operational needs in traffic management.</p></p>
