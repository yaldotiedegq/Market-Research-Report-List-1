<p><ul><li>SiO/C</li><li>Si/C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone anode material market primarily comprises two types: SiO/C (silicon oxide/carbon) and Si/C (silicon/carbon) composites. SiO/C materials enhance electrochemical performance by stabilizing silicon's volume changes during cycling, improving lifespan and efficiency. Conversely, Si/C composites leverage the high capacity of silicon combined with the conductivity of carbon, optimizing energy density and charge rates. Both types aim to advance lithium-ion battery technology for applications in electric vehicles and portable electronics, driving innovation and market growth.</p></p>
