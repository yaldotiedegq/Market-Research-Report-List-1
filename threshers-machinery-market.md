<p><ul><li>Drummy Type</li><li>Hammer Mill Type</li><li>Spike-Tooth Type</li><li>Wire-Loop Type</li><li>Axial Flow Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threshers machinery can be categorized into several types based on their design and functionality. Drummy type uses a rotating drum for threshing, efficiently separating grain from the stalk. Hammer mill type employs hammers to crush and thresh, suitable for various crops. Spike-tooth type features spikes that agitate the crop, enhancing separation. Wire-loop type utilizes wire loops for effective threshing, particularly in delicate crops. Axial flow type promotes continuous flow for higher efficiency. Other market types include variations tailored for specific crops or conditions.</p></p>
