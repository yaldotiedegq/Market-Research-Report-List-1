<p><p>The HDPE material market encompasses three primary molding techniques: blow molding, injection molding, and extrusion molding. </p><p>Blow molding creates hollow products by inflating plastic within a mold, ideal for bottles and containers. </p><p>Injection molding involves injecting molten HDPE into a mold, suitable for intricate shapes and high-volume production of items like caps and automotive parts. </p><p>Extrusion molding continuously shapes HDPE by forcing it through a die, often used for pipes, sheets, and various profiles, providing consistent cross-sectional shapes.</p></p>
