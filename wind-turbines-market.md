<p><ul><li>Asynchronous Wind Turbines</li><li>Synchronous Wind Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind turbine market is primarily divided into two types: asynchronous and synchronous wind turbines. Asynchronous wind turbines, commonly known as induction generators, operate without needing synchronized power supply and are typically less complex and more cost-effective. In contrast, synchronous wind turbines maintain a constant speed and are known for better grid stability and efficiency, but they are generally more expensive and complex. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing renewable energy generation and contributing to sustainable power solutions.</p></p>
