<p><p>The endoscopic cold light market comprises three primary types: Halogen lamps, Optical fiber cold light sources, and Xenon lamps. Halogen lamps provide consistent illumination and are cost-effective, making them popular for various procedures. Optical fiber cold light sources transmit light through fibers, ensuring minimal heat transfer and improved safety. Xenon lamps offer high-intensity light with excellent color rendering, ideal for complex surgeries. Together, these technologies enhance visibility and precision in endoscopic procedures, catering to diverse medical needs.</p></p>
